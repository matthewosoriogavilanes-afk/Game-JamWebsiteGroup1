A 2-player co-op puzzle platformer built in Unity. You and a partner control robots trapped inside a hostile automated factory. Collect scrap, upgrade your systems, and work together to break free.

// MISSION

HOW TO PLAY

⚙️

COLLECT SCRAP

Gather metal scraps scattered across the factory floors to power your upgrades and unlock new abilities.

🤖

UPGRADE YOUR BOT

Use collected scraps to enhance your robot with new tools, improved movement, and combat capabilities.

🧩

SOLVE PUZZLES

Coordinate with your partner to trigger switches, bypass hazards, and solve multi-stage factory puzzles.

CONTROLS

To control the robot, use W,A,S,D. Space bar to jump. Run over scraps to collect them.
